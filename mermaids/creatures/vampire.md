### Vampire

The Vampire is the second new Mythical Creature added in Update Version 3, alongside the [Werewolf].

#### What it does

- Transforms the player into a Vampire, swapping their model and skin over to a custom Vampire appearance.
- Lets players customize their **eye color** as a Vampire through the Vampire menu.
- Automatically hides armor and cosmetics that aren't compatible with the Vampire model while transformed, and restores them when the player transforms back.
- Grants a movement speed change while transformed, tracked and applied over time as the transformation settles in.
- Like the other creatures, transformation zones can be whitelisted or blacklisted per world/instance by a server admin.
- Can be forced on for a player by an admin, or a player can toggle whether they're allowed to become a Vampire at all.

#### How to become a Vampire

Vampire transformations are primarily potion-driven:

- **Small/Medium Vampire Potion** -- grants a temporary Vampire transformation.
- **Large Vampire Potion** -- grants a permanent Vampire transformation until removed.

Open the Vampire menu at any time with `/vampire` (or `/vamp`), or through `/mermaids vampire`.
