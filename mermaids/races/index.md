### Mythical Creatures

Starting with **Update Version 3**, the mod's transformation system was expanded into a full **Mythical Creatures** framework. On top of the original Mermaid transformation, players can now also transform into a **Werewolf** or a **Vampire**.

Each creature has its own:

- Toggle setting, so players can opt in or out of that creature individually.
- Admin command line and permissions.
- Config file with its own transformation options.
- Small, Medium, and Large potions that grant a temporary or permanent transformation.
- Custom model and cosmetic handling.

A player can only be actively transformed into one Mythical Creature at a time. Transforming into a new creature (for example, drinking a Vampire potion while already transformed into a Werewolf) will cleanly swap the player out of their previous creature model first.

#### Available Races

- [Mermaid](/mermaids/races/mermaid/) -- transform in the water for increased swim speed and underwater vision.
- [Werewolf](/mermaids/races/werewolf/) -- transform at night or during certain moon phases for a beastly form.
- [Vampire](/mermaids/races/vampire/) -- transform into an undead creature of the night with its own cosmetic handling.
