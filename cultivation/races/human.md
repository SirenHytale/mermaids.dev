### Human

> Adaptable and quick to learn. No combat extremes, but Qi flows a little more readily.

Every player starts as Human, and can choose to stay Human when the race choice unlocks at Golden Core Formation. It's the balanced, generalist option - no combat downsides, and the best raw Qi gain rate of the three built-in races.

#### Bonuses

| Bonus: | Value: |
|:---|:---|
| Unlock Realm | Body Refinement (available from the start) |
| Max Health | +0% |
| Outgoing Damage | +0% |
| Qi Gain Rate | +10% |
| Breakthrough Duration | +0% (no change) |

See the [Races](/cultivation/races/) overview for how these bonuses compare to Demon and Deity, and [Config](/cultivation/config/) for how to retune them on your own server.
