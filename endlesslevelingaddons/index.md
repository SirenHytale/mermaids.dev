### My Addons to the [Endless Leveling] Hytale Mod

**Endless Leveling Addons** are a collection of standalone race addons for the [Endless Leveling] mod. Each addon adds a brand-new playable race complete with its own ascension tree, custom passives, and stat growth. Every addon only requires Endless Leveling Core to be installed, and works independently of the base Mermaids mod, so you can mix and match whichever races you like.

Want access to every addon as soon as I release it? Subscribing to the **Siren's Endless Leveling Addons** tier on [Patreon] gives you download access to all of my Endless Leveling race addons.

You can also find a list of all my addons on my [BuiltByBit] profile.
